Much More Than Your Average Developer

Experience

User experience is the one thing a person takes away from your product. How it feels, how they remember it, and how they use your product are crucial to making their experience great.

Simplicity

If you look at the majority of websites out there, 90% of them are way too complex. That's because simplicity is very hard. You must truly understand the problems you try to solve.

Feedback

Most developers like to pretend they can do anything. But honest communication, feedback, and constructive criticism are important qualities of an exceptional team.
